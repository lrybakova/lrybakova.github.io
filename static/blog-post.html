My journeys

Iceland

Blindless is a bookless man. Icelandic proverb

Quick facts

  • Motherland of glaciers, volcanos and geizers
  • Part of Schengen area, but uses own currency
  • Whole life is concentrated by the shores, middle part is pretty wild

My tips

  • Visit either in winter or in summer, because during midseason soil becomes too muddy and many places become barely accessible
  • It is not a must to rent an SUV, roundtrip is totally doable on simple small car
  • Be ready that most of hostels have shared bathroom
  • Plan your wardrove very layered, in order to be able to adjust quickly between cold of higher areas and warmth of valleys

Finland

Work teaches the worker. Finnish proverb

Quick facts

  • Motherland of sauna, Nokia and Santa Claus
  • Part of Schengen area and uses euros
  • Famous for its nature, summer is great for hiking and lake swimming, winter for skiing and skating

My tips

  • Surprisingly it is often cheaper to stay in hotels, than airbnb, check both before booking
  • Alcohol is very expensive here and sold in special stores named Alko. Makes sense to bring own from duty free
  • Food is expensive as well, however quality is high even for the cheapest titles in groceries, don't hesitate to take them
  • As for shopping check Helsinki's main flea market, you can find scandinavian designers' clothes from 20 euros

Sweden

To lazy pigs the ground is always frozen. Swedish proverb

Quick facts

  • Motherland of Ikea, Carlson and vikings
  • Famous for highly tolerant society
  • Part of Schengen area, but uses kronas, not euros
  • Hosts headquaters of international companies for whole Nordic region

My tips

I have only been visiting Stockholm so far.

The tips about Stockholm.

Here we go!

  • Check out the subway, it is unique
  • In the early morning, when streets are empty, it is possible to take a selfie with bored palace guardian, they wear beautiful uniform!
  • Cheap way to visit Stockholm is to do a day trip by ferry from Helsinki
